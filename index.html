Harvey Milk

The most famous and most significantly open LGBT official ever elected in the United States
 

Highlights from Harvey's career

  • Served as a city supervisor in San Francisco from 1977-1978
  • Sponsored a bill banning discrimination in a wide range of scenarios
  • He was assassinated along with the mayor by another city supervisor
  • Was awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom in 2009
